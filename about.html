About us

Our company

Our company, HEINEKEN Hrvatska, is a part of the world’s most international brewing company HEINEKEN and is the second largest beer producer in Croatia. Our rich tradition dates back to 1854 and today we have a strong and diverse portfolio of high-quality products. In addition to the domestic brand Karlovačko and global one Heineken®, we produce and distribute various local, regional and international brands that satisfy the tastes of adult beer and cider lovers.
