Brewing a better world

Sustainability is the foundation of our business and something that all our brands have in common. We are determined to “Brew a Better World” by focusing our activities and efforts on six different areas that incorporate environmental protection and social responsibility.

We promote responsible consumption

  • Why?
  • How?
  • Uzivajodgovorno.hr

Why?

Beer is a natural fermented drink that, when enjoyed in moderation, can be part of a balanced lifestyle. However, when alcohol is not consumed responsibly, there are clear health and behavioural risks. For some people and on some occasions, it is better not to drink at all. We are committed to promoting responsible consumption.

How?

We actively promote responsible consumption through several initiatives and projects (such as For Responsible Youth). Heineken® is our global brand and we use its strength to show that moderate consumption is cool. In 2019, we spent 10% of total Heineken® advertising resources on promoting responsible consumption with “When you Drive, Never Drink” campaign as our focus.

Uzivajodgovorno.hr

Uzivajodgovorno.hr is a web site dedicated to practical information on how to enjoy alcohol responsibly. You can find out what it means to enjoy responsibly, get more information on the risks of alcohol consumption and learn about who should not drink alcohol at all.
